MULTI-AGENT SYSTEM

Research Pipeline

Give it a topic, and four cooperating AI agents take it from there — one searches the web, one reads the full pages behind the best results, one writes a structured report, and one critiques that report before you see it.

01

Search Agent

Scans the web for recent, reliable sources on your topic.

02

Reader Agent

Opens the top results and pulls the full page content, not just snippets.

03

Writer

Turns the raw research into a structured, readable report.

04

Critic

Scores the draft and flags what's weak before it's final.
